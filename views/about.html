What


Tomighty is a tool that helps you apply the Pomodoro Technique®. It's intended to be plain simple, easy to use and unintrusive.

Tomighty's features include:

  • Configurable times
  • Configurable sounds
  • Pomodoro™ counter
  • Displays the remaining time on tray
  • Themes
  • Multiple languages

Why

When I first tried Pomodoro™, I was amazed by how simple and effective this technique turned out to be. Results came in short time: my productivity increased and I felt a lot less exhausted. As I did not have a physical timer at hand, the natural thing to do was to search the web for some timer application. I was looking for a desktop app that lives on the system tray and does not get in they way. I tried lots of apps, but none satisfied me. That's why I created Tomighty.
