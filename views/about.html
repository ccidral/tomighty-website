What

Tomighty is a tool that helps you perform the Pomodoro Technique®. It's intended to be deadly simple, easy to use and unintrusive.

These are the current features:

  • Configurable times
  • Configurable sounds
  • Pomodoro™ counter
  • Show time on tray
  • Themes
  • Multiple languages

Why

When I first met Pomodoro™, I was amazed of how simple and effective this technique turned out to be. Results came in short time: my productivity increased and I felt a lot less exhausted. As I did not have a physical timer at hand, the natural thing I did was to search the web for some timer application. What I wanted was some desktop app that lived on the system tray and did not bother me in any way. I tried lots of apps, but none satisfied me. That's why I created Tomighty.
