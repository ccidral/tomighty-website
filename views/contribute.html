Contributing

There are so many cool things to add and improve to Tomighty, but our effort is constrained by time and sometimes skills. Maybe you want to contribute in some way.

Here goes a list of things that actually need some help with:

  • User interface design and usability
  • A new logo
  • Tray icons
  • Translation to other languages
  • Installation packages for Linux

Check out our issue tracker, there may be something else that you may find interesting.
